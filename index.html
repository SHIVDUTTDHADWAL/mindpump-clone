What exactly IS Mind Pump?


Mind Pump is an online radio show/podcast that is dedicated to providing truthful fitness and health information. It is sometimes raw, sometimes shocking and is always entertaining and helpful. Your hosts, Sal Di Stefano, Adam Schafer and Justin Andrews have over 50 years of combined fitness experience.


The fitness world is filled with charlatans and snake oil salesmen pushing the latest and greatest workout programs, supplements and faux science. They created Mind Pump as a platform to shed the light of truth on health and fitness.
